VIRAL SKIN INFECTION IDENTIFIER
Home About

This website was developed to assist users in identifying viral skin infections such as monkeypox, chickenpox, measles, cowpox, and hand-foot-and-mouth disease.

The dataset used was from Mpox Skin Lesion Dataset, which were obtained from Kaggle. The programming software used by the developers are Visual Studio Code and Google Colab.

This website was developed as a final requirement, Learning Evidence for our CSDS 3110 - Modeling and Simulation subject, as part of our third year in the Computer Science program.
